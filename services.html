    Services

    Owing to need for specialization, the firm has four department providing services in Architecture, Design, Construction and Project Management

    • 01 ARCHITECTURAL CONSULTANCY SERVICES

      Apart from meeting clients’ functional and non functional needs, our design methodologies enable us to deliver clients’ projects within cost, time and quality.

      Our design methodology includes the following;


      A. Preliminary design
      Detail analysis of clients’ brief
      Preparing and illustration of outline proposal
      Cost analysis
      Full scale presentation drawings
      Report with estimated cost for client’s approval

      B. Detail design and production drawings
      Detail working drawings with specifications
      Site plan in details
      Detail structural drawings
      Detail mechanical and electrical drawings
      Bill of quantities and cost checks
      Tender procedure/ Award

      C. Site Supervision
      Final detail drawings
      Site supervision - Site meetings/Site Visits
      Final report

      It important to note here that the above methodology enables us meet our clients’ functional requirements as well as ensuring that proposed development is delivered within cost and time 

    • 02 DESIGN CONSULTANCY SERVICES
      Design/Construction of Aviation Consoles
      Interior Design
      Modelling
    • 03 BUILDING CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

      The following are services offered by our construction department:


      Construction of Buildings
      Construction of Roads
      Water Reticulation
      Electrical works
      Erosion Control
      Landscaping
    • 04 PROJECT MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCY SERVICES

      At Shelter Consult, the management view success of Clients’ proposal as the totality of the effectiveness of managerial skills adopted from Conceptualization, Construction and throughout Life Span of the project.


      Our Project Management Consultancy Services include:
      Detail analysis of clients’ brief
      Preparing and illustration of outline proposal
      Cost analysis
      Full scale presentation drawings
      Report with estimated cost for client’s approval
      Preparation of clients’ brief
      Preparation of project feasibility study
      Advice the client on the selection of Consultants
      Advice the client on payments for work down to contractors
      Monitoring and Evaluation Consultancy and other related Project Management Services
